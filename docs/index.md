# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook, an open-source educational resource designed to provide comprehensive coverage of physical AI systems and humanoid robotics.

## About This Textbook

This textbook is organized into modules that build upon each other, taking you from fundamental concepts to advanced implementations. Each module is designed to be self-contained while contributing to the overall understanding of physical AI and humanoid robotics.

## Modules

Explore the different modules of this textbook:

1. **[Module 1: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)](./module-1-ros2/index.md)** - Introduction to ROS 2 as middleware for physical AI systems
2. **[Module 2: Physical AI and Humanoid Control](./module-2-humanoid-control/index.md)** - Physical AI foundations and humanoid robot control systems
3. **[Module 3: Perception and Sensing Systems](./module-3-perception-sensing/index.md)** - How robots perceive and interact with their environment
4. **[Module 4: Locomotion and Mobility](./module-4-locomotion-mobility/index.md)** - Locomotion principles and mobility systems for humanoid robots
